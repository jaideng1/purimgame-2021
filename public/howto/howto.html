Joining a Party

On the game page, you will come to a screen, a text box, and a couple of buttons.



On there, put your username in the box, then click on the button.



After that, you'll be brought to this screen:



A party is a group of people that you'll play with. A party can have a max of 5 people, and it's recomended that you play with 2-4 other people.


To join an existing party, ask someone in that party for their party code.
Enter that code into the box, then click to join the party.


If you want to create your own party, click to create one.



Once you've joined the party, you'll come to this screen:



To change your class, click to switch it. There are three classes - Soldier, Rabbi, and Sorcerer.


Click to leave the party.

If you are the party leader, you can click to start the game for everyone.



Once you are playing, you'll see a screen like this:



The left side of the screen is the game screen, and the right side is your hotbar and some other information.

To move, press W to go up, A to go left, S to go down, and D to go right (WASD controls). To attack, click.
If you are the Soldier class, enemies inside the triangle will be dealt damage + a bit extra.
As a Rabbi or a Sorcerer, enemies under your mouse will be dealt damage.

To change item, press 1 for the first slot, 2 for the second slot, or 3 for the third slot. The item thing is a bit glitchy, but to see the item that you have, hover over the slot to see details.

To clear a level, ✨ destroy ✨ all of the enemies in that level, then enter the brown "door".



As you progress, you'll be given random items each round, and more enemies will spawn. To get the key, get to round 10.



Are you ready to start?
